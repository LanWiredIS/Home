COMPANY PROFILE

Lan Wired Internet Services was founded in December 2020 by Froilan Garcia Isip - Sole Proprietor. since online schooling took place due to pandemic period, internet became vital to pupils and to the barangay as well that led to give free internet in the barangay and cater the school children who cannot afford to avail internet subscription.

MISSION STATEMENT

  • Provide quality, reliable, affordable internet connection and services especially among areas where internet is not accessible and available.
  • Achieve customer satisfaction.
  • Improve our services continuously.
  • Maintain professional relationships among our subscribers with our dedicated crews to achieve highest standard of performance.

VISION STATEMENT

The vision of Lan Wired Internet Services is to deliver quality and affordable internet by continuing improved services, ready for anything that may occur and quick response to customer needs and concerns regarding internet related services.

MISSION

At Lan Wired Internet Services all personnel in our company plays a vital role that lead to perform their duties to their fullest capacity and potential. Our objective is to offer quality services among our subscribers. Customer satisfaction is our main focus, reached through innovative and cost effective services.
